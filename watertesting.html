WATER TESTING







Safe drinking water and fresh air are the unavoidable needs of any living being. More than 70% of the human body is composed of water. But unfortunately, only 3% of water across the globe is freshwater and only half of that is available for humans consumption.For Water, we are offering our services for:


Dialysis Water Testing:


Near 30 times more water exposure to dialysis patients requires monitoring of water quality to avoid unknown or suspected harmful elements being carried in the water and converted to the patient. Therefore it is most important to get the water tested for meeting quality , according to the norms and conditions, specified by the health department. Water of the superior quality is used in the preparation of dialysis fluid. It is a crucial prerequisite for haemodialysis and related therapies.

Krishna Metallurgical Laboratories provide appropriate testing which makes sure the temperature level and composition of chemicals like chlorine, magnesium and calcium are relevant.
