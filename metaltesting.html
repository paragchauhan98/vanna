METAL ANALYSIS







Chemical Analysis :-


The purpose of elemental analysis is to determine the quantity of a particular element within a molecule or material. Elemental analysis can be subdivided in two ways:

  • Qualitative: determining what elements are present or the presence of a particular element.

  • Quantitative: determining how much of a particular or each element is present.
