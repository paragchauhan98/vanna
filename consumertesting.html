CONSUMER DURABLES








Washing Machines:


This established test facility covers Fully Automatic & Semi-automatic as well as Front Loading & Top-loading type. The test facility for Performance Tests covers Determination of Washing Performance; Determination of Wear Suffered by Textile; Rinsing Efficiency; Water Extracting Efficiency; Determination of Water & Energy Consumption Etc. This test facility will also address the requirements of washing machine manufacturers on obtaining the Energy Rating Labels from Bureau of Energy Efficiency(BEE) under Standards & Labeling Program of India.



Refrigerating Appliances:


Refrigerators (Direct Cool & Frost Free): Classic Calibration and Testing Lab has added another feather to its cap to have the test facility for Domestic Refrigerators in Direct Cool & Frost Free Category where it will be able to join a select group of limited test labs in India to be able to test as per the current National & International Standards including as per the key requirements of Standard & Labeling program of Bureau of Energy Efficiency.



Room Air Conditioners:


Like for the Domestic Refrigerators,Classic Calib-ration & Testing Lab has also added to its capacity to test Domestic Room Air Conditioners for limited test parameters to cover some of the specified tests. Soon it will have the complete test facility to cover all the listed tests to judge the complete performance.
